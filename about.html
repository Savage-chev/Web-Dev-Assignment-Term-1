ABOUT ME

BLOCKCHAIN

Initially, I started reading about blockchain technology and cryptocurrency in particular from investing point of view.

First time I bought cryptocurrency, Bitcoin, in particular, was 2 years ago, nearly at an all-time high. I have been following the prices and development ever since and accumulating at convenient times.

Considering every fiat currency in history has failed, the substantial raising debts, I believe the current monetary system is insufficient and is doom to fail and the cryptocurrencies are the way forward.


BODYBUILDING

With the ageing, I started to feel more and more the limitation of my body and mind. A few years ago I bought some gym equipment for home so I can exercise and improve my physical and mental health. I am not going to lie. The first 6 months was very hard until I turned it into a routine.
